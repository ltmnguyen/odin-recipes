Korean Silken Tofu


Description

Korean silken tofu can be served as a hot or cold dish. It can be eaten as a main entree or as a side dish. Korean silken tofu is savory with a soy sauce flavor, and it is made spicy with Korean red pepper flakes. It is easy to make and vegan friendly!

Ingredients

  • 1 lb silken tofu
  • 2.5 Tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 teaspoon toasted sesame oil
  • 1 teaspoon rice wine vinegar
  • 1.5 teaspoons sesame seeds (optional)
  • 2/3 teaspoon Korean red pepper flakes (gochugaru)
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • 1 stalk green onion, sliced

Steps

  1. Drain the liquid out of the tofu and place it on a paper towel
  2. To Make the Sauce: In a small bowl, mix the soy sauce, sesame oil, rice wine vinegar, sesame seeds, gochugaru, green onions, and sugar.
  3. Take the tofu and place it on a plate. Slice into rectangles and pour the sauce over the tofu.
